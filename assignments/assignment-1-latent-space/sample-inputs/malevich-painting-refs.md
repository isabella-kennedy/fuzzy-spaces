# Malevich References

For Assignment 1, please choose one of the following works by Kasimir Malevich as your "Base Sample."

## 1. Suprematist Composition: White on White (1918)
*   **Characteristics:** Subtle tonal shift, pure abstraction, infinite space.
*   **Architectural Potential:** Minimalist ground planes, subtle topographical shifts.

## 2. Suprematism (Supremus No. 58) (1916)
*   **Characteristics:** Dynamic yellow wedge, floating red/black geometries.
*   **Architectural Potential:** Aggressive cantilever, piercing circulation paths.

## 3. Black Square (1915)
*   **Characteristics:** The "Zero Point" of painting. Total absorption.
*   **Architectural Potential:** The Void, the Plinth, the absolute room.

## 4. Mystic Suprematism (Black Cross on Red Oval) (1920-22)
*   **Characteristics:** Intersection, overlay, crucifixion of form.
*   **Architectural Potential:** Crossroads, meeting points, structural junctions.
