## Learning Objective
Use **Hybrid Language** to collapse the distinctions between drawing types. You will mix your Assignment 1 result (Plan/Elevation) with patterns from Carlo Scarpa's Castelvecchio to create a drawing that reads as Plan, Section, and Axonometric simultaneously.

## The Concept: Superposition
Quantum literacy allows us to hold conflicting states in our mind at once. In this assignment, we reject the "cut" of the section line. We want a "Fuzzy Space" where the floor might also be a wall, and the plan might also be a view.
*   **Beat-Matching:** Aligning the rhythm of your Malevich massing with the intricate detailing of Scarpa.

## Instructions
