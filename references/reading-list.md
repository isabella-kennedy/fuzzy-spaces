# Reading List & References

## Primary Texts
These texts provide the theoretical foundation for "Quantum Literacy."

*   **Bühlmann, Vera.** *Atomic Time and Quantum Literacy.* In *Die Gegenständlichkeit der Information* (The Objectivity of Information).
    *   *Focus:* pp. 95–96, 104–106, 116–117.
    *   *Key Concepts:* Impersonal voice, encryption/decryption of nature, the public nature of code.

*   **Shatskikh, Aleksandra.** *The Cosmos and the Canvas: Malevich at Tate.*
    *   *Focus:* Understanding the "weightless" geometry of Suprematism.

*   **Mostafavi, Mohsen.** *Landscape as Plan: A Conversation with Zaha Hadid.*
    *   *Focus:* The relationship between the ground, the plan, and the explosion of 3D form.

*   **Nickl, Agostino.** *Navigating CLIPedia.*
    *   *Focus:* Understanding how AI models "see" and categorize semantic concepts.
